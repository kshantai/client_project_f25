Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Networking & Connections at UMSI

Networking is one of the most powerful ways UMSI students can grow academically, professionally, and personally. Every connection you make—whether with peers, alumni, or industry professionals—can open doors to mentorship, internships, and exciting career opportunities. The Career Development Office (CDO) provides guidance and resources to help you start networking with confidence. Explore meaningful connections through the UCAN UMSI University Alumni Connection Network, and check out our other CDO resources to be fully equipped for every step of your career journey.

Building a Strategic Networking Plan

Networking is one of the most powerful tools for advancing your career. To help you start confidently, the Career Development Office provides a Strategic Networking Worksheet to guide you in identifying and prioritizing organizations to work with.
