Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

Networking is one of the most powerful tools UMSI students can use to grow academically, professionally, and personally. Building connections with peers, alumni, and industry professionals opens doors to mentorship, internships, and career opportunities that often start with a simple conversation. At the Career Development Office (CDO), we offer a variety of resources and guidance to help you begin networking with confidence.

Building a Strategic Networking Plan

Networking is one of the most powerful tools for advancing your career. To help you start confidently, the Career Development Office provides a Strategic Networking Worksheet to guide you in identifying and prioritizing organizations to work with.
